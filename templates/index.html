About

Who We Are

We are a dedicated team from Holy Angel University working on cutting-edge technologies to make document scanning smarter and more accurate. Our mission is to transform handwritten documents into clean digital text using the latest OCR and NLP technologies.

Our Technology

Our app combines powerful Connected Component Analysis (CCA) with contour detection to precisely locate words in images. We also integrate TrOCR for text extraction, making it easier to convert complex documents into digital text with accuracy.

How It Works

Step 1

Upload an image using the New Upload button.

Step 2

Click the Detect Words button to allow the algorithm to detect words by line.

Step 3

Click the Extract Words button to extract the detected words from the uploaded image.

Step 4

You can save the extracted text as an Excel file by clicking Save to Excel.

Step 5

If you want to clear the workspace, click the Clear button.

Step 6

To start over with a new image, click the New Upload button again.
